## Overview

Safe simplifies finding and buying your perfect domain name. You can browse thousands of available domains, complete purchases securely, and manage ownership effortlessly. This FAQ addresses key questions on registration, post-purchase steps, troubleshooting, and support.

## Domain Registration Process

Follow these steps to register your domain on Safe.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search for Domains" icon="search">
    Enter your desired domain name in the search bar. Filter by extension like `.com`, `.io`, or `.app`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Results" icon="list">
    Browse matching domains with pricing and availability. Check WHOIS privacy options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to Cart" icon="shopping-cart">
    Select your domain and click **Add to Cart**. Review details before checkout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Purchase" icon="credit-card">
    Enter payment info and confirm. You'll receive instant confirmation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Purchase and Ownership FAQs

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="What happens after I purchase a domain?">
    Safe transfers ownership immediately. You gain full control via your dashboard.

    ```mermaid
    graph LR
        A[Purchase Complete] --> B[Email Confirmation]
        B --> C[Dashboard Access]
        C --> D[DNS Setup]
        D --> E[Live Domain]
    ```

    Update DNS records at your registrar or hosting provider to point to your site.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How do I transfer my domain out of Safe?">
    Log in to your dashboard, navigate to **Domain Management**, and generate an auth code. Submit it to your new registrar.

    Domains unlock after 60 days per ICANN rules.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All transactions use 256-bit SSL encryption.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

Resolve issues quickly based on your setup.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="DNS Propagation" icon="globe">
    DNS changes take 24-48 hours to propagate globally.

    <CodeGroup tabs="BIND,Cloudflare">
    ```javascript
    // Example A record in BIND zone file
    example.com. 3600 IN A 192.0.2.1
    ```
    ```javascript
    // Cloudflare DNS settings
    {
      "type": "A",
      "name": "example.com",
      "content": "192.0.2.1",
      "ttl": 3600
    }
    ```
    </CodeGroup>

    Use tools like `dig example.com` or online checkers.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Domain Not Resolving" icon="alert-triangle">
    Verify nameservers match Safe's: `ns1.safe.domains` and `ns2.safe.domains`.

    Clear your local DNS cache:
    ```bash
    # macOS
    sudo dscacheutil -flushcache

    # Windows
    ipconfig /flushdns
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Renewal Failed" icon="clock">
    Check billing info in your account. Renew manually via dashboard or set auto-renew.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Propagation delays are normal. Monitor with `whois yourdomain.com` for status.
</Callout>
